# CYSE 200T: Cybersecurity, Technology, and Society

To complete CYSE 200T, Cybersecurity, Technology, and Society, I learned how cybersecurity issues are connected to cultural, political, legal, ethical, and business domains. In particular, it was emphasized how all of these topics shape cybersecurity responses and decision-making. As part of this course, I wrote three short articles on cybersecurity topics, including vulnerability mitigation in critical infrastructure systems, why businesses should budget for cybersecurity training and technology upgrades, and the elements of the CIA triad.
