# IDS 300W: Interdisciplinary Theories and Concepts

By taking Interdisciplinary Theories and Concepts, I learned how to synthesize ideas from different research disciplines to formulate a cohesive research report. This enabled me to understand how to integrate research from media studies, Appalachian studies, and political science to conduct research on the impact of media representation on government investment in the Appalachian region of the United States.
