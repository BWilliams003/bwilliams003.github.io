# CYSE 201S: Cybersecurity and the Social Sciences

To complete CYSE 201S, Cybersecurity and the Social Sciences, I focused on the interdisciplinary dimensions of cybersecurity using a social science framework. By studying the social, political, legal, criminological, and economic facets of cybersecurity, I learned how these factors contribute to cyber incidents and how they are used to inform policies to reduce cyber risk. As part of this course, I produced a series of weekly journals, reviews of academic research papers that focused on social science implications in cybersecurity, and a career paper which explored the connections between cybersecurity careers and the social sciences.
