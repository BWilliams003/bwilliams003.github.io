# Artificial Intelligence

My introduction to artificial intelligence (AI) began the summer after my freshman year of college, where I interned at Kindo, a startup offering enterprise-grade security for AI, as a Customer Service Representative. It continued the summer after, where I again interned there, this time as a Cybersecurity Intern (later Cybersecurity Specialist and now Cybersecurity Engineer).

At Kindo, my focus has been on how to integrate AI to augment security workflows using both open and closed source AI models, agentic platforms, and through discussions with people in the security industry while demoing at conferences.

At ODU, I have also had the chance to conduct a bit of AI-focused research, notably in my term paper for Cryptography for Cybersecurity, which was on *Security Risks and Best Practices for Implementing the Model Context Protocol*. Read the paper and two of my featured blogs written for Kindo below. The rest of my blogs can be found [here](/blog.html).
