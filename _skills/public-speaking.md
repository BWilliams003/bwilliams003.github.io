# Public Speaking

During my time in university and while working at Kindo I have had many amazing opportunities to develop my public speaking skills. At ODU, this has taken the form of courses like Acting 1, where I memorized a monologue from *Frankenstein* and presenting various short presentations, both recorded and live. 

At Kindo, I have had the privilege of delivering talks and hosting workshops at conferences from London to San Francisco. Three of my favorites are featured below, the rest you can see <a href="/talks.html">here</a>.

      <p style="color: #6b6b6b; line-height: 1.6;">AI models trained specifically for security are here, why should devs have all the fun? Learn how WhiteRabbitNeo can help you harden your source code and improve configuration security while reducing hours of DevSecOps tedium to minutes.
