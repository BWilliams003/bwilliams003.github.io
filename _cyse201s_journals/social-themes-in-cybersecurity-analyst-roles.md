Throughout the [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iYtmuHbhmS0), one of the primary themes is that there are many different roles a cybersecurity analyst can hold. This demonstrates that it is important to be flexible and possess many of the social behaviors expected of cybersecurity professionals, as different ones may be emphasized in different roles. The cybersecurity analyst job description provided by Nicole Enesse includes an emphasis on training for user awareness and responding to social engineering attacks against your organization. Both of these components emphasize the social skill and communications social behaviors expected of cybersecurity professionals. The technical skill social behavior is also mentioned in the job description provided by Enesse, as she discusses different technical analysis roles a cybersecurity analyst may fulfill. Finally, the continued learning social behavior is important for cybersecurity analysts because the role involves researching new threats and vulnerabilities as they arise.
